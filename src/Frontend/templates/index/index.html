What is PodManager?

PodManager is a powerful podcast management platform designed to streamline the podcasting experience for creators. With features that assist in guest management, episode organization, team collaboration, task management, and audio enhancement, it's the all-in-one solution for podcasters looking to elevate their production workflow.

PodManager simplifies podcast creation and management. Whether you're a solo creator or leading a team, this platform brings all the essential tools together in one place.

It's designed to help you manage every aspect of your podcast from start to finish, ensuring efficiency and high-quality production at every step.

  • Save time with streamlined workflows
  • Improve audio quality with AI-powered tools
  • Enhance team collaboration and communication
  • Simplify guest booking and management
  • Scale your podcast production efficiently

Feature: Podcast Dashboard

Track your podcast performance with detailed analytics. Understand your audience better, see which episodes perform best, and make data-driven decisions to grow your podcast.

Feature: Episode To-Do

Stay organized and efficient with Episode To-Do, the customizable task flow that integrates all your AI editing features. Whether you need AI tasks or default actions, each task comes with built-in dependencies and easy-to-follow instructions to ensure a smooth process every time. Your workflow, your way – powered by PodManager's intuitive tools.

Feature: Guest Management

Invite and manage guests with ease. The platform integrates with your calendar to allow guests to select available times, fill out necessary information, and confirm their participation.

Feature: Team Collaboration

Create a team for your podcast and assign specific roles to each team member. Whether you need help with editing, social media management, or content creation, team collaboration is made simple

Feature: Dynamic Storage with Azure

We’ve upgraded our infrastructure for dynamic storage and resource management with Azure Blob Storage. This ensures faster, more reliable access to your podcast files, organized into easy-to-navigate folders. No more worrying about file storage limitations – just smooth, scalable podcast management.
