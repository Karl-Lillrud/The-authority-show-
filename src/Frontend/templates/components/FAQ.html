Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I get started?

Sign up on PodManager.AI for free, and you’ll have instant access to all features. Rio, your virtual AI assistant, will guide you through setup and answer any questions.

How does the AI editing feature work?

Our AI tools automatically clean up your audio by removing background noise, balancing levels, and cutting filler words—giving your podcast a professional sound with minimal effort.

What makes PodManager.AI different from other podcast tools?

PodManager.AI combines AI-powered editing, guest management, team collaboration, and workflow automation without needing extra apps or plugins. It’s designed to save you time and help you focus on creating great content.

Can I import my existing podcast episodes and projects?

Yes! You can upload your existing audio files and organize past episodes within PodManager.AI to keep everything centralized.

Can I use PodManager.AI for multiple podcasts?

Yes! You can manage unlimited shows from one account, each with its own workflows and teams.

How many team members can I add?

There’s no limit — add as many collaborators as you need to grow your podcasting team.

Can I track who’s responsible for each task?

Yes, PodManager.AI provides task assignments and progress tracking so you always know who’s doing what and when.

Can I set different permission levels for my team?

Absolutely. Assign roles like editor, producer, or guest manager with customizable permissions.

Which podcast platforms can I publish to directly?

PodManager.AI currently supports automatic publishing to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, and several others.

Can I schedule episodes to publish later?

Yes, you can set publish dates and times in advance to automate your release calendar.

Is my podcast content backed up?

Yes, all data is regularly backed up in secure cloud storage to prevent data loss.

Can I control who sees my podcasts before publishing?

Yes, you can restrict access to episodes and projects, keeping them private until you’re ready to share.

What if I encounter a technical issue?

Contact our support team via chat or email, or ask Rio, your AI assistant, for instant help.

Is there a user forum or knowledge base?

Yes! We offer a growing knowledge base and plan to launch a community forum for user discussions.
